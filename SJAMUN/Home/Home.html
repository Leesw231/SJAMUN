SJAMUN III

September 27 - 28, 2025 (SAT/SUN)

Message from the Secretary-General

Esteemed Delegates,

It is an honour to welcome you to the SJAMUN III conference. As the Secretary-General, I am excited to see the outstanding level of dedication, intelligence, and enthusiasm that each of you will bring to this forum. I expect all of you to exhibit a strong commitment to your respective countries and the issues that will be addressed during this conference.

In the coming days, you will be called upon to collaborate with your fellow delegates to draft resolutions, negotiate agreements, and build consensus. I encourage you to approach these tasks with an open mind, a spirit of compromise, and a commitment to finding common ground. Remember that the success of this conference depends on each and every one of you.
